The Method

Active Isolated Stretching (AIS)

Developed by kinesiologist Aaron L. Mattes, AIS is a scientifically validated stretching protocol that isolates individual muscles by holding each stretch for only 1.5–2 seconds, avoiding the protective reflex that limits traditional stretching.

This approach allows the muscle to truly lengthen, increases circulation to the tissue, and produces lasting neurological changes that improve posture, reduce pain, and elevate athletic capacity.

  • Precisely targets specific muscle groups
  • Avoids the stretch reflex (2-second rule)
  • Increases local circulation and oxygenation
  • Produces lasting neurological improvements in flexibility
